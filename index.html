Who We Are

Built on Compassion,
Driven by Purpose

Subaita Foundation is a registered non-profit based in Godnail, Narayanganj. Founded with a vision to uplift women and marginalized families, we have been working at the grassroots level for over a decade — because we believe that when a woman rises, her entire community rises with her.

  • Women-focused programs designed with community input
  • Partnerships with local government, NGOs & district administration
  • Transparent, accountable operations with measurable outcomes
  • Serving women across 6 upazilas of Narayanganj district

Women's Empowerment

Building confidence, agency, and leadership through advocacy programs, rights awareness, and community organizing that challenges systemic barriers.

Our Programs

Ongoing Initiatives

From grassroots workshops to district-wide campaigns, our programs deliver real, measurable impact.

01

Shokti Nari Sewing Center

Free 3-month tailoring training with job placement support. 600+ graduates so far.

02

Meye Shikhi Digital Literacy

Tablet-based training in smartphones, mobile banking, and e-commerce for rural women.

03

Alo Scholarship Program

Annual scholarships keeping 800+ girls in school by covering fees and materials.

04

Safe Home Legal Aid Cell

Free legal counsel for 300+ women facing domestic abuse or rights violations.

05

Gram Udyog Micro-Grants

Seed funding ৳5K–৳20K + mentoring. 450+ women-owned businesses launched.
