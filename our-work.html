Our Work & Focus Areas

Six interconnected pillars guiding everything we do — all centered on uplifting women and building stronger communities across Narayanganj.

Focus Areas

Six Pillars of Change

Every program we run ties back to one of these core areas — designed to create holistic, lasting impact for women and their families.

👩‍💼

Women's Empowerment

Building confidence, agency, and leadership through advocacy, rights awareness workshops, and community organizing that challenges systemic barriers and champions equality.

LeadershipAdvocacyRights Awareness
🎓

Skill Development

Vocational training in tailoring, handicrafts, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship — giving women practical tools for lasting financial independence.

TailoringDigital SkillsHandicrafts
📚

Education Support

Reducing dropout rates for girls through scholarships, tutoring, and family awareness campaigns. Education is the most powerful tool for breaking cycles of poverty.

ScholarshipsTutoringDropout Prevention
🏥

Health & Wellness

Community health camps, maternal care awareness, hygiene education, and mental wellness programs ensuring women access information and basic healthcare services.

Maternal HealthHealth CampsMental Wellness
🛡️

Social Protection

Supporting survivors of domestic violence, running child marriage prevention campaigns, and offering free legal aid for women facing rights violations or family disputes.

Legal AidGBV SupportChild Marriage Prevention
💼

Micro-Enterprise

Small grants, business mentoring, and market linkages help women launch and scale micro-enterprises — from food production to handicrafts — creating sustainable income.

Micro-GrantsMentoringMarket Linkage
