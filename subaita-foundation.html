Who We Are

Built on Compassion,
Driven by Purpose

Subaita Foundation is a registered non-profit based in Narayanganj, working to empower women through education, skills, and social support. We believe that when a woman rises, her entire community rises with her.

  • Women-focused programs designed with community input
  • Partnerships with local government, NGOs & social bodies
  • Transparent, accountable operations with measurable outcomes
  • Serving women across 6 upazilas of Narayanganj district

Our Programs

Ongoing Initiatives

From grassroots workshops to district-wide campaigns, our programs deliver real, measurable impact.

01

Shokti Nari Sewing Center

Free tailoring and garment-making training for underprivileged women, with job placement support upon completion.

02

Meye Shikhi Digital Literacy

Tablet-based digital training for rural women covering smartphones, mobile banking, and online markets.

03

Alo Scholarship Program

Annual scholarships for girl students at risk of dropping out due to financial hardship or early marriage pressure.

04

Safe Home Legal Aid Cell

Free legal counsel and support network for women experiencing domestic abuse, divorce disputes, or rights violations.

05

Gram Udyog Micro-Grants

Small business seed funding and mentoring to help women in rural areas launch handicraft, food, and service ventures.
