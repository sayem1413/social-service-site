Who We Are

A Movement Built on
Hope & Action

Asha Foundation is a registered non-profit organization that believes lasting change starts with empowered communities. We work at the grassroots level to uplift the most vulnerable.

  • Youth-led governance model with transparent operations
  • Partnerships with government bodies, NGOs & international agencies
  • Data-driven programs with measurable social outcomes
  • Operating in 14 districts with over 320 active volunteers

Our Programs

Ongoing Initiatives

From local school programs to national campaigns, our work is structured for real, lasting impact.

01

Green Campus Initiative

Transforming school environments through tree-planting, waste reduction, and eco-education modules in 60+ institutions.

02

Mind Matters Youth Program

Peer support networks and counselor training across universities and colleges to destigmatize mental health conversations.

03

She Can Lead

Leadership training and micro-grant support for women entrepreneurs in rural and semi-urban areas of Bangladesh.

04

Flood Relief Network

Coordinated emergency response for flood-affected households, providing aid to 3,000+ families in the 2024 season.

05

Digital Futures Lab

Free coding, design, and digital literacy workshops for unemployed youth aged 18–28 in underserved communities.
