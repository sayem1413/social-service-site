Our Programs

From vocational training to legal aid, our programs are designed for real women with real needs — structured for impact, measured for results.

All Active Programs

Each program is community-tested, volunteer-supported, and built to deliver lasting outcomes for women in Narayanganj.

Skill Development

Shokti Nari Sewing Center

A free 3-month vocational training program in tailoring and garment making. Graduates receive a certificate, a starter sewing kit, and job placement support. Running in 3 centers across Narayanganj.

3 Months

6 Weeks

Status
