Who We Are

Built on Compassion,
Driven by Purpose

Subaita Foundation is a registered non-profit organization based in Godnail, Narayanganj. Founded with a vision to uplift women and marginalized families, we have been working at the grassroots level for over a decade.

We believe that when a woman is educated, skilled, and empowered, her entire community transforms. Our work spans skill development, education, health awareness, social protection, and micro-enterprise support — always with women at the center.

  • Community-led governance with transparent, accountable operations
  • Partnerships with local government, NGOs & district administration
  • Programs serving women across 6 upazilas of Narayanganj
  • Dedicated team of 150+ volunteers committed to lasting change

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

💜

Dignity & Respect

We treat every woman we serve with unconditional dignity. No judgment, no barriers — only compassion and genuine support.

🌿

Community First

Our programs are shaped by the voices of the communities we serve. We listen before we act, and we act together.

🔍

Transparency

We maintain complete financial and operational transparency. Every taka raised is accounted for and directed to those who need it.

💪

Empowerment

We don't just provide aid — we build capability. Our goal is to create independence, not dependence.

🤝

Collaboration

We work with government bodies, NGOs, and community leaders because lasting change is always a collective effort.

📈

Impact Driven

Every program is tracked, measured, and refined so we continuously improve the real-world outcomes we deliver.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

🌱
Foundation Year

Subaita Foundation Established

Founded in Godnail, Narayanganj with a small team of passionate volunteers committed to women's rights and social service.

✂️
Early Programs

Shokti Nari Sewing Center Launched

Our first flagship vocational training center opened, training over 100 women in tailoring in its first year.

📚
Education Push

Alo Scholarship Program Begins

Scholarships launched for girls at risk of dropping out, keeping hundreds in school across Narayanganj district.

🏛️
Expansion

Legal Aid Cell & New Office

Established Safe Home Legal Aid Cell at Dolon Chapa City Plaza to support women in vulnerable legal situations.

📱
Digital Era

Digital Literacy Program Launched

Meye Shikhi digital training rolled out, bringing smartphones, mobile banking, and e-commerce skills to rural women.

🏆
2025

District Social Service Award

Narayanganj District Administration recognized us for outstanding contribution to women's empowerment and community development.
