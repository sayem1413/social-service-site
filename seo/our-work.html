Our Work &
Focus Areas

Six interconnected pillars that guide everything we do — all centered on uplifting women and building stronger communities across Narayanganj.

Focus Areas

Six Pillars of Change

Every program we run ties back to one of these core areas — designed to create holistic, lasting impact for women and their families.

👩‍💼

Women's Empowerment

We build confidence, agency, and leadership in women through advocacy programs, rights awareness workshops, and community organizing that challenges systemic barriers and champions equality.

LeadershipAdvocacyRights AwarenessCommunity Organizing
🎓

Skill Development

Vocational training in tailoring, handicrafts, digital literacy, and entrepreneurship — giving women the practical tools they need to earn income and achieve lasting financial independence.

Vocational TrainingDigital SkillsHandicraftsEntrepreneurship
📚

Education Support

We reduce dropout rates for girls through scholarships, tutoring, and family awareness campaigns. Education is the most powerful tool for breaking cycles of poverty and inequality.

ScholarshipsTutoringDropout PreventionFamily Engagement
🏥

Health & Wellness

Community health camps, maternal care awareness, hygiene education, and mental wellness programs that ensure women have access to information and basic healthcare services.

Maternal HealthHealth CampsMental WellnessHygiene Education
🤝

Social Protection

Supporting survivors of domestic violence, running child marriage prevention campaigns, and offering legal aid services for women facing rights violations or family disputes.

Legal AidGBV SupportChild Marriage Prevention
💼

Micro-Enterprise

Small grants, business mentoring, and market linkages help women launch and scale micro-enterprises — from food production to handicrafts — creating sustainable household income.

Micro-GrantsBusiness MentoringMarket Linkage
