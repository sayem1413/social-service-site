🔒 Subaita Foundation is dedicated to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. By using our website or services, you agree to the terms described below.

This Privacy Policy applies to all information collected through our website subaitafoundation.com, donation forms, contact forms, newsletter subscriptions, volunteer applications, and any other services we provide.

If you have any questions about this policy at any time, please contact us at mail@subaitafoundation.com.

👤
Section 01

Personal Data

Personal data is any information that can be used to identify you. We collect your data when you interact with us — online or in person — including when you make donations, subscribe to our newsletter, submit a contact or volunteer form, or attend our programs and events.

The personal data we may collect includes:

  • Full name or partial name
  • Home or mailing address
  • Email address
  • Phone or mobile number
  • Communication preferences
  • Payment or bank details (for donations only — processed securely)
  • Occupation, organization, or affiliation
  • Information about your interests relevant to our programs
  • Feedback, survey responses, and volunteer assessments

Personal data may also be provided to us directly by our staff, volunteers, campaigners, program participants, and community members during the course of their involvement with our activities.

📋
Section 02

How We Use Your Data

We use the personal information we collect only for legitimate purposes related to our mission of women's empowerment, social service, and community development. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Process donations and issue acknowledgment receipts
  • Respond to your inquiries submitted through our contact form
  • Send program updates, newsletters, and impact reports (with your consent)
  • Manage volunteer and internship applications
  • Notify you of events, campaigns, or opportunities relevant to your interests
  • Improve our website, services, and program delivery
  • Meet legal, regulatory, or reporting obligations

We will never use your data to send unsolicited commercial messages or sell advertising space. You can unsubscribe from our communications at any time.

🤝
Section 03

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted partners who assist us in website hosting, email delivery, or payment processing — bound by confidentiality agreements
  • Legal requirements: When required to comply with applicable laws or government regulations
  • With your consent: When you have explicitly agreed to sharing for a specific purpose

We may work with third-party platforms such as Facebook or Google for outreach purposes. If you interact with us through those platforms, their privacy policies also apply. We encourage you to review those policies separately.

💚
Section 04

Donations & Payment Data

When you make a donation through our website or via mobile banking (bKash, Nagad, Rocket), we collect the information necessary to process your transaction and issue an acknowledgment. This may include your name, contact details, and transaction reference.

We do not store your full bank account number, card number, or mobile banking PIN on our servers. Payment data is processed securely through the respective payment platforms.

Donation records may be retained for accounting, tax, and legal compliance purposes. We may use aggregate (non-identifiable) donation data to report on our fundraising impact publicly.

🍪
Section 05

Cookies

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit our website. We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyse how our site is used.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function properly — cannot be disabled
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand visitor behaviour (e.g. Google Analytics) — anonymised data only
  • Preference cookies: Remember settings like language or display preferences

You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies. However, some parts of our site may not function properly if you disable them. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information without your consent.

⚖️
Section 06

Your Rights

You have full rights over your personal data. At any time, you can:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct: Ask us to update or correct inaccurate information
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal data from our records
  • Withdraw consent: Opt out of marketing emails or data processing at any time
  • Object: Object to the processing of your data for specific purposes
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable, readable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at mail@subaitafoundation.com. We will respond within 7 business days.

🔒
Section 07

Data Security

We take appropriate physical, electronic, and managerial measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure. Our security practices include:

  • Secure HTTPS encryption on all web pages
  • Password-protected and access-controlled internal systems
  • Regular review of data handling practices and staff training
  • Limited access to personal data — only staff who need it for their work

While we take all reasonable steps to protect your data, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We encourage you to contact us immediately if you suspect any misuse of your information.

🔗
Section 08

Third Party Links

Our website may contain links to external websites such as Facebook, YouTube, or partner organization pages. These sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content or data practices.

We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any external website you visit through links on our site.

👧
Section 09

Children's Privacy

Our website and online services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete it from our records.

For programs that directly serve children (such as our Alo Scholarship Program), all data is collected with the consent of a parent or legal guardian.

📝
Section 10

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, legal requirements, or the services we offer. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our website after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

Have Questions About This Policy?

Our team is happy to help. Reach out and we'll respond within 1–2 business days.
