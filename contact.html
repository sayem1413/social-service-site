Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with Subaita Foundation?
You can apply through our Join Us page. Fill out the volunteer application form with your skills and availability, and our team will contact you within 3–5 business days for an orientation session.
How do I donate and how is the money used?
You can donate via bKash, Nagad, Rocket, or bank transfer through our Donate page. 96% of all donations go directly to program delivery. We publish annual financial reports for full transparency.
Who can enroll in your programs like the Sewing Center?
Our programs are open to women from Narayanganj district, especially those from low-income backgrounds. Priority is given to women with no prior income source. Contact us or visit our office to enroll.
Can businesses partner with Subaita Foundation?
Absolutely! We welcome CSR partnerships, event sponsorships, and in-kind support. Email us at mail@subaitafoundation.com or visit our Join Us page for partnership details.
Are there internship opportunities available?
Yes, we offer structured internships in social development, communications, field research, and admin. Apply through our Join Us page. We welcome students and fresh graduates with a passion for social work.
Is Subaita Foundation a Registered Foundation?
Yes, Subaita Foundation is a registered non-profit organization operating under the NGO Affairs Bureau of Bangladesh, based in Narayanganj district.
